Reflector Design for neonatal industry

The IncuWarmer reflector uses a segmented parabolic profile to evenly distribute infrared heat across the infant bed without placing the heater directly overhead. Each reflector segment is carefully angled to compensate for heat loss and ensure uniform warmth. A hyperbolic profile was also explored for a more compact, continuous design with improved heat spread.

Synthesis of Silver Nanoparticles

Synthesized SILVER nanoparticles under 100 nm using chemical method . Synthesis of silver nanoparticles is crucial due to their unique antimicrobial, optical, and catalytic properties. These nanoparticles are widely used in medicine, electronics, and environmental applications, offering innovative solutions like targeted drug delivery, improved sensors, and water purification. Their small size and high surface area enhance reactivity, making them essential in advancing nanotechnology and materials science.

Dual-Axis Solar tracker

The Dual Axis Solar Tracker automatically adjusts solar panels using light sensors and a microcontroller to follow the sun’s movement. By continuously optimizing panel orientation with two servo motors, it increases energy capture and efficiency, offering a cost-effective solution for better solar power performance.
